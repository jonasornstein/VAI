2026-07-15 — Experts Travet

What

Directory of Swedish trotting (trav) experts and outlets that publish V85 systems — full rads with spikar/garderingar — not only loose tips. Use this when you want free systems to study, named spelläggare for andelsspel, or media that builds systems live.

Why

V85 replaced classic V75 as ATG’s main Saturday multi-leg game. Systems appear in four shapes: free text/video, podcast builds, andelsspel (share of an expert’s rad), and paid tip services. Knowing who does what saves time and filters marketing noise.

5. Paid tip service (tracked packages)

  • Travtjänsten (travtjansten.se) — long-running; products like “Rank och Spel” and “Slutspelet-V85” with published result lists. Not free.

6. How systems usually appear

  1. Gratis text/video — spikar, garderingar, complete system (often small + large).
  2. Podcast — hosts build a system live (Travcash, Systemet).
  3. Andelsspel — share of expert’s full system via ATG Tillsammans.
  4. Paid service — full rads + claimed ROI tracking.
